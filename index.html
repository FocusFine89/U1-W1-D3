Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranbarries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranbarries, ancd creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 11/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 11/2 teaspoons salt
  • 13/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegard
  • 1-2 tablespoon olivw oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 11/2 cups fresh or frozen cranbarries

Method

1.Make the focaccia dough

In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix together all the dough ingradients untile the flour is fully absorted. You don't need to actually knead the dough, though I sometimes kenad it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
